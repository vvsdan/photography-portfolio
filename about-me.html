Hi there! I'm Dan!

Welcome to my photography portfolio! With over 5 years of experience, I specialize in capturing the essence of individuals through portraits.

Armed with a Bachelor of Science degree and a minor in psychology from the University of Waterloo, I infuse my work with a deep understanding of human behavior and emotion.

Whether it's a professional headshot, or a creative editorial shoot, I am dedicated to providing exceptional service and timeless images that exceed expectations. Outside of photography, I'm an aspiring frontend developer, constantly expanding my skills and exploring new technologies.

This multifaceted approach to creativity allows me to bring a unique perspective to my work and adapt to the evolving needs of my clients. If you're seeking a versatile and talented photographer who can capture your essence with precision and artistry, look no further. Let's collaborate and create something extraordinary together.
